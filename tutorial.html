    Heart Disease Prediction

    Choose one of the two methods below to predict the risk of heart disease: by analyzing heart sounds or using personal health parameters.

    Option 1: Predict Using a Heart Sound File

    This method analyzes the acoustic properties of heart sounds to detect potential anomalies. A trained AI model listens to the sound file and identifies irregularities that could indicate conditions like arrhythmias or murmurs.

    Use Sound Prediction

    Steps to Record and Analyze Your Heartbeat:

    Step 1: Record Your Heartbeat Use your smartphone or a digital stethoscope to record your heartbeat.

    Ensure that you are in a quiet room and record at least 10 seconds of clear heart sounds.

    Step 2: Save the File

    Save the recording in a supported format (e.g., .mp3, .wav).

    Step 3: Upload the File

    Click the 'Upload Sound File' button to submit your recording.

    Step 4: Analyze the Results

    The AI model will process the sound and display the prediction of potential heart diseases.

    Option 2: Predict Using Health Parameters

    By entering various key health metrics such as age, gender, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, and lifestyle factors (e.g., smoking or exercise), an AI model predicts your risk of heart disease based on clinical data patterns.

    Use Clinical Assessment

    Steps to Predict Using Health Parameters:

    Step 1: Collect All Required Health Reports

    Gather your latest reports for cholesterol levels, blood pressure, ECG results, and any other relevant medical information.

    Step 2: Enter Your Parameters

    Fill in your health details, including age, gender, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, smoking habits, and physical activity levels in the provided fields.

    Step 3: Click the 'Predict' Button

    After entering your details, click the 'Predict' button to let the AI analyze the data.

    Step 4: Review the Results

    The AI model will provide a detailed report, indicating your risk level for heart disease along with any recommended actions.
